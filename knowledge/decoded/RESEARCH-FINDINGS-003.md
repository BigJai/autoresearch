### Why This Works (And Fixed Days Don't)
- Saturn's daily motion is NOT constant — it varies from ~0.03°/day to ~0.13°/day
- During retrograde periods, Saturn effectively "pauses" then reverses
- So "360°" of Saturn motion ≠ a fixed number of calendar days
- The average is ~53 days for commodities, but individual instances vary from ~30 to ~90 days
- This explains why fixed 30-day cycles showed only 75% alternation last session
- **The clock is planetary, not calendrical**
