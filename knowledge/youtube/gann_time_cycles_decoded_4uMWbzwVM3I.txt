# Gann Time Cycles Decoded — How They Actually Work
# Source: YouTube video 4uMWbzwVM3I (Gann Trading channel, John)
# KEY INSIGHTS for autoresearch

## 4 Elements Required for Time Cycle Work:
1. PLANET or PLANETS (sidereal for single, synodic for pairs)
2. CYCLE LENGTH — IN DEGREES not days!
3. STARTING POINT / ANCHOR — must backtest to find correct one
4. CORRECT RATIOS — the rhythm of the cycle (H-L-H-L pattern)

## Critical Rule: Use DEGREES not DAYS
- Days will drift over time, degrees are precise
- Even 0.1 degree matters for shorter cycles
- "If you use days instead of degrees your cycle won't work properly after a short period of time"

## Lunar Cycle Example:
- Sidereal value of Moon = 27.321 days (NOT 29.5 synodic phase cycle)
- Common mistake: using lunar phase (29.5d) instead of sidereal (27.3d)

## How Ratios Work:
- Example: length=180°, ratios=50° and 40°
- Sequence: 0→50° (low)→90° (high)→140° (low)→180° (high) = end of cycle
- The H-L-H-L alternation comes FROM the ratios, not random
- Same ratios keep repeating within the cycle

## Cycle Wrapping:
- When cycle length exceeds 360° (e.g., 630°), it wraps around the circle
- 630° = one full revolution (360°) + 270° more
- New cycle starts at end of previous: 630°→1260°→1890°...
- "That process will continue till the last day the market stands"

## Key Quotes:
- "If you are highly dedicated and hardworking you may find the planet and the length
  but you won't be able to find the anchor and the ratios"
- "With the correct ratios you will be able to forecast with up to 90% accuracy"

  agrees to teach you"
